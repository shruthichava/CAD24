// readme.md

Build the thing you keep talking about.

Grab a team (or find one here), pick a track, and CAD out a working demo in 24 hours. We supply the space, the snacks, the prizes, and mentors/guest speakers.

// pick_a_track

Four ways to hack.

Choose one at check-in. Switching mid-event is allowed and mildly encouraged.

  • Aerospace: Build for aviation, space, or satellites.
  • Biomed: Solve healthcare and biotechnology challenges.
  • Robotics: Design autonomous or mechanical systems.
  • Sustainability: Create solutions for climate and the environment.
